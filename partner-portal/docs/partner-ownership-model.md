# Partner Ownership Model

Indicative model:

- Local partner typically retains 65%-75%.
- SymbioGreens/Balponics typically retains 25%-35%.
- Preferred maximum SymbioGreens/Balponics participation is 35%.

This is not fixed or guaranteed. Final structure depends on project contribution, territory, financing, operational role, risk, legal structure, and negotiation.

The portal includes a visual JV simulator for 25%, 30%, and 35% platform participation.
