# Partner Market Survey Toolkit

The market toolkit helps partners validate:

- Hotels and resorts
- Restaurants and chefs
- Supermarkets
- Distributors
- Hospitals
- Schools
- Mining camps
- Government buyers
- NGOs and food security programs

Outputs:

- Buyer readiness score
- Buyer interview checklist
- Crop demand validation
- Price validation direction
- Cold-chain assessment prompt
- Recommended next step
