# Partner Financing Pathway

Potential pathways to explore:

- Local bank financing
- Development finance
- Government agriculture programs
- Climate resilience grants
- Food security programs
- Impact investment
- Hotel/offtaker-backed financing
- Diaspora investment
- Equipment financing
- Renewable energy financing

No financing is guaranteed. Every pathway is subject to country, eligibility, documentation, lender/program rules, offtakers, and project readiness.
