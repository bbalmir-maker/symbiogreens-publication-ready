# Rainwater Capture Calculator

Formula:

```text
annual_capture_liters = rainfall_mm * catchment_area_m2 * runoff_coefficient
```

Example:

```text
1,200 mm * 1,000 m2 * 0.8 = 960,000 liters/year
```

Outputs:

- Liters per year
- Liters per month
- Liters per day
- Suggested storage target by days of buffer

This does not replace engineering, water quality, drainage, storage, or treatment review.
