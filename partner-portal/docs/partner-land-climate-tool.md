# Land And Climate Tool

Inputs:

- Country, region, latitude, longitude
- Altitude
- Land size
- Usable percentage
- Greenhouse footprint
- Rainfall
- Sun hours
- Water and electricity assumptions

Outputs:

- Suggested production footprint
- Support area
- Expansion reserve
- Project scale
- Water and energy strategy notes

Future API candidates:

- NASA POWER
- Open-Meteo
- Local meteorological services
