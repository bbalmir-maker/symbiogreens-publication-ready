# Investor Email Invitation Flow

## Approval Email

Subject: SymbioGreens investor review access approved

Body should include:

- Confirmation that the investor has been approved for private review access.
- Account setup link.
- Reminder that materials are confidential and subject to review terms.
- Notice that materials are illustrative, non-binding, and not a securities offer.

## Pending Email

Subject: SymbioGreens investor review request received

Body should include:

- Confirmation of receipt.
- Expected review timeline.
- Reminder that private materials are provided only after approval.

## Declined Or Deferred Email

Subject: SymbioGreens investor review request update

Body should stay concise and professional, with no disclosure of private materials.

## Implementation Notes

Email sending should happen through a backend function or trusted email service. Do not send privileged email API keys from browser code.
