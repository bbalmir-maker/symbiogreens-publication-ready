The private portal may discuss possible expansion pathways for the Dominican Republic, Haiti, the Caribbean, Africa, joint ventures, technical services, and renewable-integrated food production hubs.

## Important Limits

All expansion content must remain:

- Illustrative.
- Non-binding.
- Not guaranteed.
- Subject to legal review.
- Subject to local partner validation.
- Subject to financing, operational, regulatory, and market risk.

## Public Site Boundary

The public SymbioGreens site should not expose private expansion details, private calculations, investor review tools, engagement scoring, admin analytics, or private document previews.
